About us

For some activities, like driving a car or flying to Kenya, it’s easy to understand the resulting carbon footprint. Over the last few decades, we have been focused on transportation as the only significant driver of climate change.
But it’s time to take a more thorough approach, and understanding our digital carbon footprint is a good place to start. The first step in reversing climate change is understanding the actual impact that we are having on our environment. With our Carbon Footprint Calculator, you can gain a more thorough understanding of your own personal CO2 emissions. Even better, you’ll be able to directly invest in an 8 Billion Trees forest carbon project, therefore negating as much CO2 from your carbon footprint as you’d like.
